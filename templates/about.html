About GTS notifier

GTS notifier is a service that will send you notifications through pushover when your trades on Pokemon X and Y's global trade station have been successful. You can receive these notifications using the pushover clients, available on both desktop and mobile.

New trades are checked for every hour. Higher limits are redundant, since the global link website updates GTS trades at an unknown interval much greater than an hour. This is a problem on the global link website's end and can not be fixed by me.
